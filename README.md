
# AI-Based Smart Helmet for 2-Wheeler Safety 🏍️

### KPIT Sparkle 2026 Project

**Problem Statement:**  
India reports 11% of global road deaths with 44% involving two-wheelers due to delayed emergency response, drunk driving, blind spots, and theft.

**Our Solution:**  
IoT + AI based Smart Helmet with 4 integrated safety modules:

1. **Accident Detection & Emergency Alert** - MPU-6050 accelerometer detects crashes >4G force. After 10 seconds, SIM800L GSM sends SMS with GPS location to family & 108 emergency services.
2. **Drunk Prevention System** - MQ-3 alcohol sensor blocks vehicle ignition if BAC exceeds 0.03%.
3. **Blind Spot Alert** - Ultrasonic sensors detect vehicles within 2 meters and trigger LED + voice alerts.
4. **Anti-Theft Tracking** - GPS module sends real-time location alerts on unauthorized movement.

**Tech Stack:** Arduino Nano, SIM800L GSM, GPS Neo-6M, MPU-6050, MQ-3, Ultrasonic HC-SR04, Embedded C  
**Cost:** Under Rs 2500 | **Impact:** Addresses 80% of 2-wheeler fatality causes
